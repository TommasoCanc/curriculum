About me

I'm an ecologist passionate about understanding biodiversity through big data, spatial ecology, and ecological modeling. My work focuses on uncovering patterns in species distribution and ecosystem responses to global change, integrating taxonomy, traits, and genetics. I lead a multidisciplinary team as Head of the Data Management and Analysis Unit, where I drive innovation in ecological research through software development, data infrastructure, and collaborative workflows. Committed to open science, I develop tools in R and Python to make research more efficient, accessible, and impactful.
