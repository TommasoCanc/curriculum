Profile

Ecologist with extensive experience in biodiversity big-data analysis, spatial ecology, and ecological modeling. Skilled in integrating large-scale datasets to uncover patterns in species distribution and global change.

As Head of the Data Management and Analysis Unit, I have developed strong leadership skills and the ability to lead a multidisciplinary team, from biologists to software engineers. I like to identify opportunities to enhance team productivity, streamline workflows, and foster collaborative innovation in ecological and data science.

I am a strong advocate for open science and collaborative research, committed to advancing environmental sustainability through good science and innovative technological solutions.
